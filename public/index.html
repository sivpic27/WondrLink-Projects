Important Information

WondrLink is a health education tool designed specifically for colon cancer patients and their caregivers.

This tool provides information to help you understand your diagnosis, treatment options, and care journey. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult your healthcare team for medical decisions. If you use our screening tools (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS-10, ISI), please share your results with your healthcare provider.

Privacy Policy · Terms of Use

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Information We Collect

WondrLink collects the following information to provide personalized cancer care guidance:

  • Account information (email address for authentication)
  • Patient profile data you voluntarily provide (diagnosis, treatments, biomarkers)
  • Chat conversation history

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized, evidence-based cancer care information
  • To match you with relevant clinical trials
  • To improve response quality and accuracy

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted databases. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Conversations and profile data are associated with your account and are not accessible to other users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal health information with third parties. Your data is used solely to provide and improve the WondrLink service.

Your Rights

  • You can view and update your profile at any time
  • You can clear your conversation history
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting support

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact our team through the WondrLink support channel.

Terms of Use

Effective Date: February 2026

Purpose

WondrLink is an educational support tool designed to help colon cancer patients understand their diagnosis, treatment options, and care journey using evidence-based medical guidelines.

Not Medical Advice

WondrLink does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. All information is for educational and informational purposes only. Always consult your oncologist or healthcare team before making any medical decisions.

Accuracy

While WondrLink draws from established medical guidelines, AI-generated responses may occasionally contain errors. Always verify important information with your healthcare provider.

Clinical Trials

Clinical trial information is sourced from ClinicalTrials.gov. WondrLink provides trial matches as a convenience; eligibility must be confirmed by the trial's research team.

Emergency Situations

WondrLink is not an emergency service. If you experience severe symptoms, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate profile information for the best experience
  • Do not rely on WondrLink as your sole source of medical information
  • Consult your healthcare team for all medical decisions
